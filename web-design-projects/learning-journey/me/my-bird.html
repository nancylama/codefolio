Boogie

Boogie was my blue parakeet. I had her for eight years before she passed away recently, but I still love showing her off.

Boogie's only pastime was causing trouble. Her favorite things included:

  • Biting me
  • Screaming for no reason
  • Sleeping on the toaster
  • Looking at herself in any reflective surface
  • Pooping everywhere but her cage
  • Sitting on my head and eating my hair
